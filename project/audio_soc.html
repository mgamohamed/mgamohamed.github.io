Audio SoC

I specialize in designing data paths for professional audio codecs, focusing on reducing artifacts and enhancing audio fidelity. With extensive experience in signal processing and digital design, I create solutions that elevate audio quality to new heights.

Expertise in Audio Signal Processing

My work in designing data paths for pro-audio codecs encompasses:

  • Interpolation and Decimation Filter Chains: Developing high-performance filter chains for ADC and DAC to maintain audio quality and preseve signal integrity without introducing artifacts.
  • Equalizers:Designing advanced equalizers that precisely adjust audio frequencies to enhance sound quality and meet specific audio requirements. My expertise ensures minimal distortion and optimal performance across diverse audio applications.
  • Total Harmonic Distortion (THDN) Improvement: Developing a novel algorithm to reduce the artifactes created from the data converters. I also optimized the algorithm for hardware implementation for low latency, low power and small area.
  • Dithering: Developing a multi bit LFSR to dither the data path in order to overcome the quantisation noise and keep a noise floor with white nature.
  • Dynamic Element Mismatch: Developing a DEM to be implemented with data converters to overcome the element mismatch.
  • Filters: Designing various filters, including low-pass, high-pass, band-pass, and notch filters, to shape the audio signal precisely.

Signal Processing with MATLAB

Signal processing is at the core of my data path design:

  • Simulation and Analysis: Simulating the entire signal processing chain to preemptively address potential issues.
  • Algorithm Development: Crafting custom algorithms, such as adaptive filters and equalizers, to meet unique project requirements.
  • Optimization: Continuously refining techniques to balance computational efficiency and audio quality.

Digital Design Excellence

My expertise extends to the digital design of the data path:

  • Hardware Implementation: Translating signal processing algorithms into HDLs for implementation on FPGAs and ASICs, optimized for performance, power consumption, and area.
  • Pipelined Data Path Architecture: Developed a new pipelined data path for higher resuability and customization.
  • Verification and Testing: Employing rigorous verification and testing protocols to ensure reliability and performance.
